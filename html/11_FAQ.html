Frequently Asked Questions
How will I know about my selection?
You will get mail from us, If you get selected and will receive an invitation to the telegram group.
What is the batch duration?
Each batch lasts for a month and consists of 3-4 courses of 7-10 days per course.
What is the criterion for selection as a participant?
Your devotion and desire to learn is the only criteria for selection.
Which countries and states are eligible to participate in the program?
People from every country and state are welcome to participate in this program.
Is it strictly for girls or can boys also participate?
GirlScript seeks to help anyone who is new to the field of development, and it promotes an inclusive environment in particular. As a result, EOP is open to both girls and boys. Everyone is welcome to participate and learn.
How will participants communicate with mentors and admins?
All course-related communication will take place in the telegram channel.
Is there any application/registration fee?
No, it is free for all.
Who all are eligible to participate?
Anybody who is willing to learn new skills and willing to mentor the participants are eligible to participate
