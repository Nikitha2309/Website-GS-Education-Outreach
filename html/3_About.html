About Education Outreach
Education Outreach Program is an initiative of GirlScript Foundation where we conduct 7-day live technical and non-technical courses and organize sessions for those who can strike us with their passion to reach the zenith.
Till now we have impacted 25,000+ students in our Education Outreach Program. This program runs all year, and each batch includes a variety of technical and non-technical courses. Every course is 7 days long, and our mentor teaches Live.
